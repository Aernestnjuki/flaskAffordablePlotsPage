How to Invest

Now I know this might be a very hard question, but to help you understand ask yourself if you are looking for speculation purposes or your aim is to build your home. Speculation is a case where you just want to invest and resell later at a better price.

How you look and where do you start? The easiest way is to go online and look for the track record of different companies. That is, Do they deliver what they say? Are they registered? Do they have a permanent physical place where they can be reached? These are the question you should start with then move to the next.

Make sure you have visited their office to find out more information. Then schedule for a site visit. Now while at site make sure you have seen permanent beacons, and make a point of asking around in the community about the land to secure your investment. Also make sure you have asked for the title deed before placing any deposit.

The main purpose of a title deed search is to confirm the land's owner and also to if the land is in the general map of the area. This is important as you do not want to deposit your money on someone else land. If the land is not registered in the name of the person or company selling, then its wise not to continue with the transaction.

After making sure that everything is in order, choose the best payment plan that works for you and then place a deposit. Then you will have to sign the sales agreement which is a legal document that contains all the necessary information about the purchase and terms and conditions. Make sure you understand and agree to everything in the sales agreement.

The last step after completion of payment, the vendor should provide ypu with the transfer and consent forms with are used in the land register to register the title deed in your name. After that wait for the title to be processed in your name. Now you know every step for investing in land.
